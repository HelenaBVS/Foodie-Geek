Welcome to Foodie Geek!

At Foodie Geek, we’re not just about recipes – we’re about the science behind the food you eat. Whether you’re exploring the world of gluten-free cooking out of necessity or curiosity, we believe that understanding the "why" behind each recipe can elevate your culinary experience.

Our recipes are meticulously crafted to be not only delicious but also scientifically sound. Ever wondered why certain gluten-free flours behave differently? Or how to achieve the perfect texture in your baked goods without gluten? We delve into the chemistry of ingredients, explaining how they interact to create the foods you love.

But it’s not all about the science – food is also culture, history, and emotion. We sprinkle in fun facts and cultural context to give you a deeper appreciation of the dishes you create. From the origins of traditional recipes to their modern, gluten-free adaptations, we explore it all. Join us on a journey where science meets the kitchen, and let’s cook something amazing together!
